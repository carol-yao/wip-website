Snag

Development • Aug 19 - 21 2016

STACK

Ruby on Rails • React • Twitter API • Indico API

OUR TEAM

Snag was built at Hack the 6ix in late August 2016 in a team of 3. We all came from very diverse backgrounds: a graduated chemical engineer, a student studying chemical engineering and myself, a biomedical science student. We met at Bitmaker, a coding bootcamp program for people who wanted to transition into a technical career and decided to go to this hackathon as our program was ending to apply everything we learned.

OVERVIEW

We used a React front end and a Rails backend to build out a chrome extension tool that produced a feed of potential customers for the marketing team to reach out to. We narrowed our scope to find users that struggled with personal finance for the Wealthsimple challenge at the hackathon.

DEVELOPMENT & MY ROLE

We retrieved tweets using the Twitter API and ran them through the Indico emotion APIs to determine which users were in need of financial help. We packed this feed into a chrome extension to simplify usage for marketing teams to use as a targeted advertising tool. I worked mainly on retrieving the tweets and running them through the Indico APIs for analyzing the topic, tone, and sentiment of the tweet.
