Development & Design • Oct 29 - 30 2016

STACK

Unity Game Engine

OUR TEAM

Defender was built at GameJam2016 in October at the University of Waterloo in a team of four university students who never worked with Unity Game Engine before. We had a fantastic time learning about game development and testing our game!

DEVELOPMENT & MY ROLE

My role was developing the health scoring system and point system. Heart containers would decrease when asteroids hit the earth. However, there were different asteroid types that had varying damage, speed and HP. I learned a lot from this hackathon about game development and found Unity very friendly to use. We had plans of making a mini store at the end of each game to buy powerups with points earned but we ran out of time ☹️.
